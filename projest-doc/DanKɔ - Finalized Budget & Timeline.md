## 8. Cost Optimization Options

### To Reduce Budget to ~GHS 250,000:

1. **Use Junior Developers** - Save GHS 30,000-40,000
2. **Skip VoIP Initially** - Save GHS 10,000-15,000
3. **Use Free Tiers** - Analytics, error tracking - Save GHS 5,000
4. **Reduce Marketing** - Focus on organic - Save GHS 20,000
5. **Part-time QA** - Owner does initial testing - Save GHS 10,000

### To Accelerate Timeline to 4 Months:

1. **Add Second Flutter Developer** - +GHS 50,000
2. **Parallel Backend/Frontend Work** - Requires coordination
3. **Use UI Component Library** - Skip custom design
